Memory is a communal act.

The La Casa HP&E Archive is built by students, alumni, and community members. We are looking for documents, photographs, flyers, and oral histories that reflect the vibrant life of the Latine community at Yale since 1969.

Whether you have a single photo from a meeting or a box of records from a student organization, your contribution helps us remember properly.
