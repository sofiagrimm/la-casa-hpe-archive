The digital hearth.

The HP&E Hub is a central resource for all historical preservation and education efforts within La Casa Cultural. It serves as both a repository for digitized records and a platform for new research.

By centralizing our history, we ensure that the labor of past generations is not lost to the passage of time or the transience of student life.
