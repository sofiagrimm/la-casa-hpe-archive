The collective memory.

MEChA de Yale

A student organization dedicated to Chicanx culture, history, and activism on campus since the late 60s.

La Casa Cultural

The Latinx Cultural Center at Yale, serving as a hub for community building and historical preservation.

Ballet Folklórico de Yale

Preserving and sharing traditional Mexican dance through performance and community engagement.

GRADLAS

Yale Graduate Students of Latin American and Caribbean Origin, bridging the gap between undergraduate and graduate communities.

Sabrosura

Yale’s premier Latin dance team, contributing to the vibrant cultural landscape of the university.

Despierta Boricua

Yale’s Puerto Rican student organization, founded in 1971 and dedicated to promoting Puerto Rican culture and advocacy.

UndocuAllied at Yale

Supporting undocumented students and allies through advocacy, resources, and community building at Yale.

La Unidad de Yale

A Latinx political and cultural organization advocating for the rights and visibility of the Latine community.

Yale Maracatu

An Afro-Brazilian percussion and dance ensemble celebrating the cultural traditions of the African diaspora in Latin America.

Alianza for Latin America at Yale

Promoting awareness of political, social, and economic issues in Latin America through events and advocacy.

Latinx Pre-Professional Society

Supporting Latine students in pre-medical, pre-law, and other professional tracks through mentorship and networking.

HP&E Committee

La Casa’s Historical Preservation & Education committee, dedicated to archiving and sharing the community’s history.
