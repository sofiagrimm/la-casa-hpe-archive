Mission

Keep it accessible. Keep it real.

The La Casa HP&E Archive is a community-centered project dedicated to preserving the history of Latine life at Yale. We believe that history is not just found in textbooks, but in the everyday experiences, activism, and joy of our community.

Our archive is built for students, alumni, and community members to explore their heritage and ensure that future generations can learn from the labor and triumphs of those who came before.

Multimedia Focus

We collect photographs, oral histories, audio clips, flyers, and documents to provide a rich, multi-layered view of our community’s past.

Radical Accessibility

Every label and prompt is designed for clarity, ensuring that even a first-year student can navigate the archive with ease.

Living Archive

The archive grows as the community contributes. Submit photographs, oral histories, or documents and become part of the permanent record.
