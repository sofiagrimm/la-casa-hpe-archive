# La Casa HP&E Archive

A digital community archive concept for La Casa Cultural's Historical Preservation & Education (HP&E) committee at Yale University.

## What this is

A multi-page HTML prototype that models a community-centered digital archive. It includes:

- Multimedia collections browser (photos, audio, video, flyers, oral histories)
- Interactive Yale memory map concept with Yale-specific landmarks
- Layered timeline system (1969, 1974, 1990s, 2019)
- Affiliated group mini-archive framework (MEChA, Ballet Folklórico, Sabrosura, etc.)
- Public contribution flow via email redirects
- Forest green and gold visual system
- Fully responsive multi-page layout

## Design System

- **Background**: Deep Forest Green `#1a2e1f`
- **Surface**: Surface Cards `#223327`
- **Text**: Cream `#f2ead8`
- **Muted**: Sage `#9aab97`
- **Accent**: Gold `#c9a84c`
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (Headers), Space Mono (Body/UI)

## Structure

- `index.html`: Homepage and portal
- `mission.html`: Project mission and ethics
- `collections.html`: Multimedia archive browser
- `map.html`: Geographic history of presence at Yale
- `timeline.html`: Historical timeline of advocacy and growth
- `groups.html`: Student group mini-archives
- `submit.html`: Contribution guidelines and contact
